Name: The Wedding of Fish
Translation Source: Wesleyan University
Artist: Rukmini Chitrakar

Text:
Let’s go Rangila for Daria fish’s wedding. Let’s go Rangila for Daria fish’s wedding
Katla fish says—I’ll go with the palanquin o Rangila. Let’s go Rangila for Daria fish’s wedding
Taroi fish says—I’ll play the wedding shehnai o Rangila. Let’s go Rangila for Daria fish’s wedding
Shol fish says I’ll take a pot of sweets o Rangila. Let’s go Rangila for Daria fish’s wedding
Magur (catfish) says I’ll play the guitar o Rangila. Let’s go Rangila for Daria fish’s wedding
Singi fish says I’ll play the sitar o Rangila. Let’s go Rangila for Daria fish’s wedding
Chanda fish (pomfret) says I’ll be your ear rings o Rangila. Let’s go Rangila for Daria fish’s wedding
Ganga fish says I’ll be the ornament on your forehead o Rangila. Let’s go Rangila for Daria fish’s wedding
Prawn says I’ll play the harmonium in the wedding o Rangila. Let’s go Rangila for Daria fish’s wedding
All the fishes say—we’ll eat at your wedding o Rangila. Let’s go Rangila for Daria fish’s wedding
Boyal fish says I’ll eat the entire fish salas (sister fuckers) o Rangila. Let’s go Rangila for Daria fish’s wedding
